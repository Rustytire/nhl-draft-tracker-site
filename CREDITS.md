# Credits & Attribution

Board Battles measures and grades other people's work. None of the rankings
below were produced by this project — they belong to the analysts and outlets
named here, and we are grateful for them. Our contribution is the measurement,
the grades, and the blended consensus we compute *about* these boards.

**Principle:** derive, don't reproduce. We publish the new thing we made (a
grade, a correlation, a consensus). We link back to every source so readers go
*to* them. Paywalled work is never reproduced as an ordered board or exact rank.
See `docs/data-ethics-and-paywall-policy.md`.

## Draft outcomes & player value
- **Hockey-Reference / Sports Reference** — draft results, games played, points,
  and Point Shares (the basis of the Value axis). Used under their terms; not
  redistributed here.
- **NHL.com / NHL Central Scouting** — official draft records and CSS rankings.

## Paid / subscription sources — measured, credited, never reproduced
These sources sell their boards. We grade them and count their contribution to
consensus, but we **never** publish their ordered board or exact ranks — only
derived measurements, coarse ranges where public coverage is sufficient, and a
link to the original. Please subscribe to and support their work:
- The Athletic — **Corey Pronman** & **Scott Wheeler** (theathletic.com)
